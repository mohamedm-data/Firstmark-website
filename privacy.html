Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Firstmark Media Co ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website or engage our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect contact information you provide voluntarily (name, email address, phone number, company name), information submitted through our contact forms or booking system, technical data such as IP address and browser type collected via analytics tools, and communications you send us directly.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, schedule discovery calls, deliver our services, improve our website, send service-related communications, and comply with legal obligations. We never send unsolicited marketing.

Meta Ads and Data Handling

We operate Facebook and Instagram advertising campaigns on behalf of clients via Meta's platform. We do not sell or share individual lead data outside the client relationship. All ad spend flows directly to Meta. We act as an authorized manager strictly in accordance with Meta's Terms of Service.

Cookies

Our website uses analytics cookies to understand visitor behaviour. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though some functionality may be affected.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers under strict confidentiality agreements, or when required by law.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold. Contact us at hello@firstmarkmedia.co.
